How to play Minesweeper

Upon first click, 7 mines will be generated at random into an 8 x 8 grid.

  • Numbers: A number will appear on available squares dictating how many mines that square is in contact with.
  • Left click: Squares can be uncovered with the left click, if you click on a mine the game ends. The goal of the game is to uncover all the non-mine squares.

Note: the game is won by uncovering all the empty spaces
